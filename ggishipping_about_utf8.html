About Us

Established in the year 2009 by a dynamic entrepreneurs, Parvender Singh, GGI Shipping Agency is a one-stop integrated logistics and transportation service provider, offering a comprehensive range of high quality solutions in shipping transportation segment. With a wealth of experience and expertise in various facets of shipping transportation segment, GGI Shipping is ideally placed to offer solutions that will give our clients a competitive advantage in navigating complex global shipping environment.

Our solutions touches upon a host of areas ranging from Transport Operations to Freight booking, Project Forwarding, Documentation and shipment of Hazardous cargo, etc. Leveraging our good network of contacts, we are well positioned to provide our clients easy access to international ship owners, operators, and charters. This in turn helps our clients to extract greater cost-efficiencies in freight, etc.

In line with our resolve to stay a step ahead of competition, our team keeps track of market trends and conditions and focus on keeping our customers satisfied in every aspect of our services. It is this spirit of commitment and our flexible approach that makes us an ideal source you can rely upon for the success of your requirements.

Looking ahead, we aim to carve a niche for ourselves as a market leader that’s recognized for providing result-oriented customized solutions
